    zondacrypto Trading Platform

    The zondacrypto Trading Platform is a fast-moving marketplace where experienced users trade crypto directly with others. Prices are shaped by speed (whoever will be first), as well as market demand and depth (the amount of available offers).​​​​

    ZND Platform

    For beginners, the ZND Platform offers a simpler approach with ZND Trade, which lets you buy and sell crypto at fixed prices that are visible upfront. You can also manage your assets with ZND Wallet, and grow them passively with ZND Staking and ZND Earn.

    zondacrypto pay – for businesses

    Let your customers pay in crypto

    Let your customers pay in crypto

    zondacrypto pay payments gateway lets sellers accept crypto in return for their products and services. Customers can pay with over 10 popular crypto-assets, while you automatically receive money in your bank account in EUR or PLN.
    It is a settlement solution intended for business use. Transaction processing times can differ depending on the crypto-asset used. Please check the Table of fees and commissions and zondacrypto pay website to learn more about the service.

    Get started in 5 steps

    1 Create an account

    Enter and verify your email address and phone number. You will receive from us a message with a verification code. Finally, set a strong password for your account.

    2 Verify your identity

    KYC identity verification is a standard procedure in any regulated cryptocurrency exchange. It ensures the security of transactions - for both buyers and sellers. After successful verification, you can fully use all the exchange functions. The process takes only a few minutes!

    3 Secure your account with 2-Factor Authentication

    This is a step required to use zondacrypto services. Use one of the 2FA methods we provide to access your account securely. One recommended method is using the free Google Authenticator to protect your money and personal information better.

    4 Deposit funds

    Choose the traditional currency (PLN, EUR, USD) or cryptocurrency in which you want to deposit funds. You can deposit via bank card or bank transfer, with options for traditional or instant processing.

    5 Start trading crypto

    Log in to your account and start investing. Our site offers blog posts and Academy lessons to help you build an impressive cryptocurrency portfolio.
